Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Fine Living Guide uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand how our content is used.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences and provide analytics data to help us improve the experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand visitor behavior. All data is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users.
  • Preferences: Cookies remember your acceptance of this notice so we don't show it repeatedly.
  • Affiliate tracking: When you click affiliate links (Amazon Associates, Awin network partners), those services may place their own cookies to track referrals.

Third-Party Cookies

Our affiliate partners including Amazon Associates may set cookies when you click links on our site. These are subject to their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Your Choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some site functionality. You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
